About Me

Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Geoffrey Li. I am a recent graduate of computer engineering program at UofT. I have worked as an creator of automated testing suites for several companies. I am also a budding web developer, starting to experiment creating simple and intuitive static and dynamic websites.

Paymentus

At Paymentus, I helped design an automated testing suite to follow best practices such as the use of page object models. I designed testcases for testing non browser aspects of paymentus services such as Interactive voice reponse (IVR solutions) and testing rest api functionality.

Bluecat Networks

During my time at Bluecat, I worked as a Test Automation Engineer with Agile Scrum Teams to design, develop, and maintain the automated testing suite for Bluecats' IP Address Manager(IPAM) web tool using the web browser automation tool Selenium Webdriver. I also coordinated with developers and QA engineers to identify, report and fix bugs, defects and other issues.

Celestica

I worked at Celestica during my Professional Experience Year (PEY) term. I created macros in visual basic to automate elements of electrical component analysis and report generation.
